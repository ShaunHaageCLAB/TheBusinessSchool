It’s always in your best interest to register without delay as space is limited. To secure yourself a space, you can apply online or in person:

Online: If you’re keen on finding out more about the programmes you can fill out a general enquiry about programmes, on our website.

To register for a short learning programme, please complete and return the following registration form to the campus you want to attend with accompanying documentation via email or fax to the campus you would like to register with.

To register for a part-time programme, please complete and return the following registration form to the campus you want to attend with accompanying documentation via email or fax to the campus you would like to register with.

Before sending, please ensure you have done the following:

  • The form must be completed in black ink.
  • The account payer must initial the bottom of each page and all areas specified.
  • Please provide your full signatures on the back page.
  • Certified copies of original documents must be included. Note that foreign students and South African students will be required to provide different documents. Please check that you have supplied the correct ones.

In person: For short learning programmes, submit this registration form to The Business School

For part-time learning programmes, submit this registration form to The Business School

Before submitting this, please ensure you have done the following:

  • The form must be completed in black ink.
  • The account payer must initial the bottom of each page and all areas specified.

Please provide your full signatures on the back page.

Certified copies of original documents must be included. Note that foreign students and South African students will be required to provide different documents. Please check that you have supplied the correct ones.
